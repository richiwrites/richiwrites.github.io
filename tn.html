Art

Happiness March 25,2021

richi.writes
Stop what you are doing. Tell me this. Why did you stop making art? When did you start thinking "life" is more important than art? Why do you now put less time into what you love and more into what makes you stressed and why do you go to sleep wondering if you wasted the day?

Art. What is art, ...after all? I would say art is what you dream of. Why did you stop dreaming? Was it because someone told you to make your art about "real" things or was it because someone told you your art was too complicated to understand? Is this why you now put less time into painting, singing, dancing, writing or simply doing something you love? Did someone tell you "life" is more important than "art?"

I'm sorry you heard that. I'm sorry the world stopped you right when your heart was full of excitement. Please don't stop believing in your art. I'll look at a painting of a street cat with equal amazement as how I'll look at an abstract painting I won't probably understand the meaning of. I'll read a whole 31 line poetry about post-breakup phase with equal interest as I'll put into an article about spaceships that I have no clue about. I'll listen to an original singer with equal feelings as I'll listen to the improvised version of a cover. If you're the artist and making art makes you happy, your art form to me is a whole universe waiting to be discovered. Your art is the whole world inside you. Your art is you. Your art will remind me of the sunflowers and will suffocate my lungs with joy and make me happy to be alive to witness this. Your art will make me fall in love. No matter how "complicated" or "unreal" your art form is, your art is the weight of all the things you carry with you the world never asked you about. Your art reminds me of happiness.

Beloved

One sided March 31,2021

richi.writes Dear my beloved's lover,

I have written to you quite a lot of times before, they're just letters I never sent
But today I'm writing you for the last time, I hope you read it to the end

Dear my beloved's lover,
I wonder what it's like to have him all by ...yourself, that's a feeling I never will know
I have lots of questions but questions I don't want you to answer though
I wonder what it's like to touch his dimple while he smiles
I wonder how it's like to hold his hand and walk miles after miles
I wonder if you like watching him play on the ground as much as I do
I wonder what it's like to hear him call your name when he misses you
I wonder what his favourite colour is and if yours matches with his
I wonder if you two hum songs in the evening over two cups of teas
It's okay I don't want the answers, they're answers I don't want to know
I'm writing you this today because maybe it's my time to let him go
It's okay we had our time and now it's time for me to leave
It's okay my deadline has been met and he's all yours to keep
I would tell you to love him the way I would if I could
But no, love him your way but love him right cause if I could, I would

Dear my beloved's lover,
When things get hard and the world stands on his opposite side
I hope you stand stiff next to him and for him I want you to fight
I hope you smile every morning you wake up to his face
I hope you two find home in each other's arms after long, exhausting days
When his tongue rolls in the softness of your name, I hope you remember how it feels
I'll be a chapter long closed, a forgotten name in the home you built

But that's okay
I'm writing you this with a heavy heart and tears in my eyes
But know your love came to you at the cost of my hardest goodbyes
I'm writing you this because I want you to promise me this
In some other life, you'll let him be mine and I'll finally be his
This time, in this life, I want you to promise me you'll never love him in halves
Because I'm handing over my heart to you, with a tag "he's all yours to love"

Signing off,
Someone who fell for the same eyes as you did
