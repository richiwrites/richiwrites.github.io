Bully

Stop July 9,2020

richi.writes
My parents wondered why I hated going to school
That's because I was bullied for not being "beautiful"
I had an "ugly" skin tone, a darker shade of brown
I was told I'm "dirty" and I was treated like a clown
People laughed at me and the boy I liked never liked... me back
And this way I grew up focusing on only what I "lack"
I lathered my skin with fairness creams and I got those acnes and rash
I didn't care because I wanted to be treated as something more than "trash"
And till date, I never dare to go out without makeup on my face
School still haunts me and it was supposed to be my "safe" place.

I was bullied at school because I was "fat"
I will never ever forget the way I was laughed at
I was poked with pencils and was called the ugliest names
Tell me honestly, was my overweight really my matter of shame?
Not only the students, the teachers too called me "the fat kid"
Teachers who taught me humanity in class themselves forgot about it
I too wished I had friends with who I could sit, laugh and talk
But I knew I was "fat", an object only to be mocked
Trust me tried my best to jerk off my shame, I ate less to not at all
And till date I wish "fat" wasn't the only name I was called.

I was bullied online no matter what dress I wore
I could never be pretty, all I was called were "slut" or "whore"
Random people commenting on my pictures saying they wish I died
But it hurt so much some nights, I remember how hard I cried
They caused my clinical depression and I was tired of fighting beasts
But I forgave them the moment I put the blade on my wrist.

Dear parents, don't tell your sons crying makes them weak
Hug them tight and listen to them while they speak
Teach your children to always value the people around them
Tell them if someone is being bad, they're not the ones to blame
Teach your children to respect, love and care
And tell your kids the words they still need to hear
"It's normal to be bullied on social media, comeon get in the line"
CYBER BULLYING IS NOT NORMAL, STOP SPREADING HATE ONLINE
I hope the children grow up being taught love instead of hate
Because school is supposed to be a "second home" for kids, not a place they dread.

La Vie En Rose

Memories July 14,2020

richi.writes
Lying under the night sky, I see a shooting star, you know

I wonder if some other star cried while letting this star go

I wonder if stars on the sky fall in ...love too

I wonder if they love like me and leave like you

I still remember your presence smelled something like hope

I still remember your presence felt something like home

In your presence, my wounds started to heal

But now I'm forgetting how your presence used to feel

I chased your memories and locked them in cages

In my diaries with their old yellowed pages

I read our memories, the remnants of a past I left behind

Each word laced with your presence makes me want to rewind

To go back in time and ask you to stay

So you and I could have just one more day

I wish for one more day I could look into your eyes

I wish for one more rain with you under the sky

And I love you still each time the sky turns grey

And I will love you always each time la vie en rose will play

And as cliche and hopelessly romantic it sounds,

I will love you till the last drop of rain touches the ground

And some nights like this, I selfishly wish you stayed

There are still feelings left to be felt and things left to be said

I'm grateful though, that you stayed as long as you did

You made me believe even two parallel lines can meet

We were two parallel rays but our paths did cross

And the memories with you make up for all that I've lost

The smiles with you make up for all the tears I've shed

I let you go with grace, with smiles, but I still wish you stayed

Couldn't you stay, just a little longer, maybe?

After you, life ain't the same, but I'm fine, you see

And each monsoon, I will walk on the streets under the rain

Smiling with the memories of rains with you, wishing I could go back again

There are so, so many things worth living for

Yet here I live just to love you more

But the shooting star was never meant for the star on the sky

And i wonder if it's tears of that star everytime the sky cries

And I still have one more thing left to say to you

It was hard loving you, you know, but it was harder not to

Future Love

Love Life July 17,2020

richi.writes
Dear future lover,
Even though we have no idea about each other's existence now,
Someday soon, we'll be in love with each other
We'll do all the things people in love do
And Once I'm in love again, I'm not letting you go
But before that, here's something I want you to... know

Chances are, like Most love stories, ours too will start with a "replied to your story"
But tell me your stories
No, not the instagram ones that last for only 24 hours, Tell me stories of your life.
I promise your snapchat dog filter is really cute
But give me your school ID card version.
You'll probably be making those cute playdate edits with my videos
But know that I'll be waiting for "din shagna da" at our wedding
I know a lifetime isn’t enough to know a person
But we'll probably meet by swiping left On hundreds of uninteresting bios on tinder.
I will be watching lots of your sky photographies on your Instagram stories
But come, Let's watch the sky together.
I want your thoughts to pop up in my head every second
Your chat head can just chill.
Let's not talk about those 1k+ facebook friends
Let's talk about the friendships we've lost along the way.
You might be storying your tired face with a time sticker of 2 am but let's talk about what keeps you awake all night.
Enough of playing it cool by sharing depressed quotes and single memes
Let's sit on the rooftop at 2 am and talk about that one heartbreak we can't get over

Dear future love,
Save that money instead of buying me expensive presents
Your presence Will mean the world.
Candlelight dinners in posh restaurants can wait
Let's have dinner at my place in those steel plates with lentil and rice.
We can always fight over the last slice of pizza
But Let me snatch that one fuchka from your plate.
Conversations over 800 tk crimson cup coffees can wait
Let's have little chitchats in the balcony sipping our "extra milk" evening teas.

Give me the typical 90s bollywood romance
Give me the love that manages family finances
Give me the love that chooses maggie over ramen
Give me the effortless, fearless, unquenchable kind of love.
Because my love, enough of scrolling, typing and swiping
It's time we love looking into eyes instead of screens

Feminism

Respect women July 28,2020

richi.writes
How many times have we hesitated to call ourseleves a feminist?
It's okay, it's a difficult time for the society and females to coexist
In a country where women are being "favoured" by being "allowed" to learn
As if our right to survival and education were prizes we need to earn
How many times do we get asked "are you those feminist type?"
Feminism is not a trend we follow, it's actually... different than you describe
You'll be sitting in a roadside stall with your friends, sipping your tea
While you state that a world without women would be a better place to be
You don't want your girlfriend to wear a croptop but youll visit restricted sites at nights
You'll share her private photos in group chats but won't have a share in the battles she fights
You show us our place is in the kitchen to hold a spatula and a knife
But you often seem to forget the knife that I hold might also take your life
I am a woman, a mother,a daughter I'm all this and more
I have the softest of hearts, I'm also a tigress that roars
I define feminism by the women who decide to speak, feel and think
Feminism is blue, black, red and every color and no, it doesn't resent pink
Feminism is Malala Yousafzai who took a bullet in her head for female eduaction
Feminism is the girl that refuses to marry wherever dowry is mentioned
Feminism is Begum Rokeya for whom today I speak, feel and write
Feminism believes my education is never my privilege but simply my right
Feminism is a MAN questioning patriarchy and its crooked mentality
Feminism is every human being who stands against injustice, who calls for equality
Feminism is the girl with a zeal to learn but can't make it to school
Because being a female is apparently against the "right to education" rules
Feminism is every woman that cries when clothing is blamed for rape
Feminism is the goddess you lower your head before, a woman you worship to find escape
Feminism is a woman who smiles, screams, cries, falls but gets up too
Feminism is us, feminism is me, feminism is you
Enough of suppressing our voices and oppressing our feelings unspoken
Women,it's time to choose the paths not chosen and to take the roads not taken
